# Research

### Research interests

Combinatorial optimization: single and set tolerances, Minimal Spanning Tree Problem, Traveling Salesman Problem. 

Algebraic graph theory: strongly regular graphs, Deza graphs, divisible design graphs, equitable partitions of graphs, eigenfunctions of graphs, enumeration of graphs.
